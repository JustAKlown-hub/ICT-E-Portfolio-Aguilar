Reflection
I learned the importance of Filipino culture and why it should be celebrated. A Filipino culture I do is being respectful to my elders and speaking the Filipino language. Unfortunately, I wasn't active enough but I did wear "Barong Tagalog" to school every monday and participated for the declamation elimination. If I were to teach my friend about "Buwan ng Wika", I would show the values of a Filipino and show them the culture of Filipinos,either through example or verbal. I believe it is good having a month dedicated to Filipino culture, with this we will learn and enjoy our culture together
